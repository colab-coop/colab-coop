Finding his way from the snowy tundra of Plattsburgh, NY eight years ago, Jayme arrived in CoLab’s home base of Ithaca. Upon arriving in Ithaca, Jayme noticed the lack of a local underground music scene and created Ithacaunderground. The first thing he did was create a website.

Jayme graduated from SUNY Plattsburgh with a bachelor’s in philosophy with a minor in anthropology. A self-taught developer, Jayme has been working on websites for nearly 15 years, "since before websites had backgrounds," and has seen the Internet grow and change. His main impetus is to create and explore new technologies. His current interests are the JavaScript-based development tools Backbone and Angular.

Recently Jayme embarked on his most ambitious project yet: fatherhood. Or in his own words, "the hardest, most rewarding job I have ever had."
