Sabin Vaccine Institute

Health advocacy

This project was brought to CoLab through Echo & Co, who have been designing participatory campaigns and building technology tools that make the world a better place since 2003. We partnered with Echo & Co and Sabin as the development team, executing the site design on the open source Drupal platform. The site features a gorgeously bold slide show allowing nearly any content to be promoted to the front page. It also features a data ticker that allows Sabin to display results of the organizations’ efforts throughout the site.

There are more than 2 billion people who live on less than $1.25 per day, putting them at risk for contracting vaccine-preventable and neglected tropical diseases. The good news is that through Sabin’s programs diverse partnerships, vaccines and treatments are delivered to people in developing nations who need it most. If you are interested in saving lives, look to the Sabin Vaccine Institute for inspiration.
