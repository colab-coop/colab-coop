Ralph leads branding and marketing at CoLab. A trained percussionist and former professional musician, Ralph spent years as the marketing director at a music label in New York before co-founding CoLab.

Ralph graduated from Ithaca College with a bachelor’s in communications. Since his early days tinkering with Legos, Ralph has sought to build a better way of doing things. His mission has frequently taken him off the beaten path, from Amsterdam to Montreal to Africa.

Now he brings the same unconventional sensibility when partnering with CoLab’s clients. He focus is combining original design concepts with sustainable, long-term solutions. Ralph enjoys producing music and spending time with his wife and two sons.
