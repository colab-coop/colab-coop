Project Manager

The role of Project Manager is multifaceted — much like spinning a bunch of plates (that are on fire) simultaneously. The position requires the ability to interact professionally in writing or on the phone with our clients. This position requires someone who is very organized and detail oriented. This position also requires someone who can recognize possible roadblocks or issues and work to resolve them before they get out of hand.

Experience and Requirements
  • 1-3 years experience with project management or similar
  • Available and "on call" when needed per contractor agreement
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Self starter
  • Strong professional communication skills (verbal and written)
  • Interest in highly collaborative environment
  • Driven to make the world better for everyone
  • Experience with project and task management systems
  • Experience with time tracking and invoicing systems
Other things we're looking for
  • Experience facilitating agile development processes
  • Technical knowledge related to web and mobile application development
  • Familiarity with Atlassian’s Jira and Freshbooks software
  • Keen design eye and appreciation of simple beauty a big plus
Role and Responsibilities
  • Display strong Project Management capabilities, including team communication facilitation
  • Facilitation of team check-ins / scrums
  • Tracking of open loops / important communication threads
  • Skillful communication with clients
  • Watch-dogging budgets / percent complete on projects vs. estimates
  • Setting and tracking progress against key project milestones
  • Track and route incoming support requests effectively
  • Display ability to unblock critical work through proactive communication and project vision
  • Assist in tracking team capacity
  • Help with documenting project requirements for new work requests
  • Assist in defining and documenting project work processes on company wiki
  • Ability to support and help with QA and functional testing
Time Commitment

20-30 hours a week to start.

Location

Flexible.

Compensation

Commensurate with experience.
