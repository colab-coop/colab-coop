At the heart of your success is digital expertise that cares.

Operating as a worker-owned cooperative, CoLab is an agile development shop and an international, cross-cultural team of technology visionaries.

Our people include DSchool faculty from Stanford University and members who have served prominent governmental organizations (US Census Bureau, NY Public Library), universities (Cornell University, UC Berkeley), and non-profits (National Resources Defense Council, Sutter Health, Search Inside Yourself Leadership Institute).

Passionate people are our process.

We use a number of processes, each as varied as the needs of our clients and the personalities on your team. We work with you to develop a process that's right for your project. The path might change, but our dedication and high standard of service never do.

Understand your vision

We will most often seek to understand "why" before talking about "how."

Create value with minimal investment

We will work to conserve your resources until a high level of confidence is achieved.

Test your assumptions

We will assist in finding the assumption most vital to your business and develop creative, efficient ways to test them.

Keep in close contact

We will be there, proactively establishing next steps, giving financial updates, and providing recommendations intended to fortify and focus the energy propelling the project.

Collaborate

Collaboration with you and each other is pivotal to our process. We will draw on our wide variety of talented specialists for advisement and production work.

Assemble a killer team

We will assemble a team of specialists tailored to your specific needs.

Conduct agile sprints

The team will meet with you weekly to go over assigned tasks, dates for specific deliveries, and address budgetary updates.

Keep you updated

In addition to consistent check-ins with project managers, you will have access to project status and details in online organizational systems.

Present multiple solution options

We will present alternative solutions so that you can choose the tactics that are most aligned with your vision. Ultimately, what we do is up to you.

Reflect

We may request a reflection meeting after a delivery phase has been completed. We want to hear about your experience working with us. Your feedback helps us continually grow and improve.
