Kube Website and Application Hosting

What is Kube?

Kube is a hosting solution designed to make deployment and management of applications seamless and efficient. Because Kube runs on container software, you'll enjoy a turn-key solution for your staging and production environments. No more worrying about the complicated setup and maintenance of your infrastructure.

Kube provides dedicated hosting services for small organizations (co-ops) and computational infrastructure for larger organizations. Kube is for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

What does Kube include?

Kube provides out of the box:

  • Turn-key deployment of applications using YAML configuration files for streamlined and efficient setup
  • Preconfigured staging and production environments, ready for use with just a few clicks
  • Seamless deployment of applications with automatic scaling, resource allocation, and management
  • Regular, automatic backups to ensure your data is secure and easily recoverable in case of any issues
  • Human-centric support

In simple terms, Kube simplifies the process of deploying and managing applications, so you can focus on creating amazing software.
