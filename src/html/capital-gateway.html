Do co-ops need better investment and mentorship? Do you?

CoLab and the International Cooperative Alliance are exploring what it might look like to build a service to for co-ops.

Our research has found that co-op entrepreneurs struggle accessing and using capital strategically. Stakeholders on all sides of cooperative development say they want investment to strengthen, not weaken, a co-op and its principles. But finding relationships for mentorship and assistance to help make smart use of financial capital is difficult for them, too.

Help us turn our research into a service that connects co-ops with an ecosystem of investment and mentorship in valuable ways.
