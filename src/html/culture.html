At the heart of your success is digital expertise that cares.

CoLab Coop is a smart, meaning-rich, company who by virtue of its "do good" culture and deep technical expertise is home to the world's top engineering talent.

Operating as a worker-owned cooperative, CoLab is an agile development shop and an international, cross-cultural team of technology visionaries. We engage with our investors, peers, partners, and community members through a deeply collaborative process that is industry-leading in its approach. Our approach is grounded in leading research in achievement of organizational excellence referencing Lean Startup methodologies, collaborative workplace theory, flow state achievement, and meaning quotient enhancements: all in service to innovation, productivity, and impact.

The team on deck to work on your project is quite simply, world-class. Our team includes DSchool faculty from Stanford University, members who have served the needs of large governmental organizations (US Census Bureau, NY Public Library), the largest and brightest American universities (Cornell University, UC Berkeley), large non-profits (Sabin International, National Resources Defence Council, Oceana.org, Sutter Health, Search Inside Yourself Leadership Institute) as well as entrepreneurial technology startups which you likely haven't yet heard of (but which we hope you soon will know well!).

We are thought leaders in our respective fields, presenting at major technology conferences and contributing code to ground-breaking open-source projects like Derby and Drupal.

Together we amplify the impact of our technology ventures, advising, and the execution of development of new technology solutions for mission driven organizations: from social enterprises to innovative non-profits and foundations to businesses supporting the local economy.

It is through working together in deep collaboration that the most effective and innovative solutions can be be developed to solve the world's problems, and we have built our business from the ground up to do just that.

We're on a mission.

CoLab helps businesses harness the power of the technology to serve humanity and the planet.

We've got history.

CoLab was born in 2011 by a group of creatives, technologists and entrepreneurs. The founders began cultivating their relationships to one another over a decade ago. Fostering each others' individual growth and serving others were central to these friendships. This trusting foundation became part of the fabric of what is now CoLab Coop. Being of freelance and entrepreneurial backgrounds, CoLab sought a way to create opportunities to do what they loved - exploring technology, social progress, design, music and spirituality - while helping others to achieve their own dreams.

This path forward, together, is shared now through the cooperative business model as co-owners of CoLab Coop. We discovered that a coop enables each of us as worker-owners the freedom and responsibility to create an organization that reflects a shared set of core values rooted in a desire to make the world a better place.
