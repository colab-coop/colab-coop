User Experience Designer

CoLab is seeking to build a pool of Designers to bring on to projects and work within our project teams. Currently we are searching for user research and user experience design expertise. As a User Experience Designer you will play a crucial role in shaping the user journey and overall experience for our clients digital platforms. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional, international teams, including project managers, account managers, engineers, and architects to design intuitive and visually appealing digital solutions that meet client requirements while delighting users.

CoLab works at the intersection of cooperation, inclusion, and technology. We have a long history of collaboration with clients committed to economic justice, democratic principles, and environmental sustainability. We’re seeking collaborators with at least 3+ years job or freelance experience designing for web and/or software applications.

This is a part-time, flexible, and remote position. Initially, the projects will be intermittent and you may only have one project at a time. Over time, you will be offered more and more opportunities with CoLab. You may decide to become a co-op member-owner and start participating in internal business or innovation projects.

If this resonates with you, please read on.

Responsibilities
  • Lead a user-centered design process by gathering user insights, conducting user research, facilitating workshops, and translating findings into actionable design improvements.
  • Create wireframes, clickable prototypes, and user flows to visualize and communicate design concepts effectively.
  • Develop high-fidelity mockups and visual assets that maintain a consistent and visually pleasing design language. Collaborate with the development team to design interactive elements that enhance the user experience and contribute to a seamless product journey.
  • Plan and conduct usability testing sessions, stakeholder interviews, and user interviews. Synthesize feedback and suggest design refinement based on user input.
  • Work closely with project managers and engineers to ensure the feasibility and successful implementation of design solutions. Ability to estimate, work with, and communicate around budgets.
  • Establish or build upon design guidelines, patterns, and libraries to ensure smooth development and design consistency across different platforms and features.
  • Demonstrate a firm understanding of accessibility standards and design principles while keeping up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and design best practices to bring innovative ideas to the team.
Qualifications
  • A portfolio demonstrating a solid understanding of user-centered design principles, showcasing a range of design projects across different platforms.
  • At least 3 years of experience as a UX/UI designer, preferably working on complex digital products or applications. Ability to produce foundational design assets such as wireframes, user flows, and journey maps.
  • Understanding of modern design tools and experience creating interactive prototypes. Fluency in the tools CoLab uses is a big plus (Figma, Miro, Adobe Creative Suite, Framer).
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
  • Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to distill complex problems into elegant and intuitive design solutions.
  • Deep understanding of user needs and motivations, and a passion for creating products that enhance user experiences.
  • Ability to adjust plans and methodology based on unique situations, changing priorities, and project requirements.
  • A keen eye for detail, ensuring design accuracy and consistency.
Other things that are helpful
  • Education: Bachelor's or Master's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field.
  • Coding Skills: Ability to do basic front-end development is a plus.
  • Participatory Practices: Experience with participatory design, design thinking and inclusive, reciprocal approaches during product discovery phases
  • Content Strategy: Firm grasp of content strategy for marketing and search engine optimization. Experience creating deliverables like information architectures, sitemaps and primitive wireframes.
  • Familiarity with hybrid/native UI development frameworks such as React Native, Chakra, etc.

If you do not perfectly meet the requirements and qualifications described above, but you are excited by meaningful work, a cooperative workplace and think you would be able to fill this role with excellence and joy, please apply!

Location Requirement

We are a global cooperative, and this position is open to applicants from all regions. We currently have members in North America, Europe, India, and South-East Asia. Our working hours are mainly +/- 3 hours Eastern time zone.

Compensation

Our cooperative has recently passed a comprehensive approach to rate standards for all of our members and collaborators. The hourly rate for this role will be determined based on a standard set of merit and experience-based criteria, applied equally to all members of our workplace.

Time Commitment

Hours range, up to 10 hours per week to start. Hours are expected to increase over time in accordance with worker availability and project needs.

About CoLab

CoLab is an international, worker-owned tech collective that builds custom apps and websites for a better world. Our co-op was founded in 2010 to co-create simple, purpose driven solutions to global problems. We run a very open, flat, and collaborative organization, and invest in one another as friends and coworkers. Our co-op has an open path to becoming a member-ownership, which we hope you’ll consider.

Who We’re Looking For

CoLab coworkers bring a wide range of talent and perspectives to our collaboration. We’re looking for people who try to meet complexity with simplicity, and can communicate about it with their peers. We are looking for great listeners, who can uncover needs and creatively find solutions. We are looking for excellent collaborators who share our values of Innovation, Collaboration, Passion, Wisdom and Sustainability.

As a co-op, we embrace both autonomy and mutual aid. You’ll have clear responsibilities in your role, as well as opportunities to contribute in other areas. We have no set criteria around particular background or credentials; we care about your proposals, what you produce, and your growth and happiness with our team.
