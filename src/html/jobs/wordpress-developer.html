WordPress Developer

CoLab is seeking an organized, enthusiastic and quick-to-learn individual to develop and build WordPress sites that and help us continually evolve our do-good tech cooperative so we can help more people! The Ops Support collaborator is an junior to mid-level position focused.

We’re a tech company so comfort with technology is obvious, but we are also a worker-owned cooperative. We’re a close-knit group who are exploring a new way to do business: one based on cooperation, collaboration and a high-integrity desire to shape a better world. If that excited you, reach out!

Experience and requirements
  • 2-4 years experience developing WordPress sites
  • Translate visual comps into pixel-perfect modern, responsive, and semantic HTML & CSS
  • Integrate dynamic content into sites using JQuery and JQuery extensions
  • Research optimal solutions for problems faced by our clients
  • Available and 'on call' when needed per contractor agreement
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Self starter
  • Team player (you’ll be part of the Delivery Team and work closely with PMs, designers and other developers)
  • Strong professional communication skills (verbal and written)
  • Interest in highly collaborative environment
  • Driven to make the world better for everyone
  • Experience with time tracking and invoicing systems
  • Ability to interface with clients
  • Ability to manage and communicate timelines, scope and budget
  • Experience working in a remote, distributed team a bonus
Other things that are helpful
  • Experience participated in team development processes and scrums
  • Technical knowledge related to web and mobile application development
  • Fully comfortable using GIT and the git-flow methodology
  • Familiarity with Jira, Trello, Google Apps, HipChat, and Freshbooks software a plus
  • Appreciation of simple beauty and clean code a big plus
Role and Responsibilities
  • Help with documenting project requirements
  • Ability to support and help with QA as needed
  • Attend project meetings to update project status with Project Manager
Time Commitment

Project based 15-20 hours a week to start

Location Requirement

Flexible

Compensation

Commensurate with experience

Benefits
  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly peers
  • Participate in Do Good projects
  • Lots of room to grow
  • Educational resources
  • Stretch responsibilities that challenge you to level up!
  • Path to ownership
  • Value-aligned community
  • Respect for work / life balance
  • Support for spiritual growth
Ownership Candidacy

A path to ownership is open for the candidate. Details are based upon current membership criteria and subject to approval from the Board of Directors.
