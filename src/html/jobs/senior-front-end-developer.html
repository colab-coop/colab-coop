Senior Front-End Developer

CoLab is a worker-owned tech collective that builds custom apps and websites. We’re seeking an experienced front-end developer, architect, and team lead who works well in a collaborative environment. Our main client for this hire is a well-known company that produces eco-friendly household products.

Requirements
  • Comprehensive knowledge of front-end development principles: architectural considerations, best practices, and modern design patterns.
  • Twig, Drupal or PHP theming experience strongly preferred. Experience in other templating languages with excellence in results and demonstrated rapid ability to learn will also be taken into consideration.
  • Responsive front-end development skills, with ability to review and work off of designs to develop standards-aligned, front-end code, and to execute pixel-perfect implementation from comps as needed.
  • Experience with PR review process, git (ideally gitflow) and workflow based on JIRA or other ticket-based project management toolset.
  • Comfort with ES6 Javascript and ability to work with JavaScript code.
  • Knowledge of modular CSS practices, including familiarity with SASS or a similar preprocessor.
Also Great
  • Familiarity with PatternLab or other component-driven design and development frameworks and processes.
  • Front-end leadership experience or demonstrated ability in the following areas: interface with designers and back-end development teams, support collaborating front-end developers, document processes and decisions, and discuss approaches and problem solving with clients.
  • Knowledge of Drupal coding standards for basic module and theme development.
  • Experience working within non-hierarchical teams, cooperatives, or other collaborative structures.
  • Experience working with mission-driven organizations: B-corporation, social-impact start-ups, community organizations, cooperatives, etc.
  • Minimum 3-4 years experience front-end development with Drupal sites using CSS, Javascript, and HTML.
Time Commitment

This position requires about 30-35 hours/week. Our working hours are +/- 3 hours eastern time zone. This initial engagement lasts through May 2018, with strong potential for long-term work across a variety of clients. The ideal candidate is interested in pursuing membership with CoLab’s cooperative structure, but this is not a requirement.

Team working hours are +/- 3 hours eastern time zone.

Location Requirement

Remote position. We have collaborators in more than two dozen cities worldwide! Position is open to applicants living in Alabama, California, Massachusetts, New York, Ohio, Oregon, Texas, Vermont, Washington State, Wisconsin or outside of the U.S.

Compensation

Hourly, Commensurate with experience

Apply

Please email jobs@colab.coop with an cover letter and resume, relevant professional links, code samples, and availability, with the subject line “CoLab Front-End Application.” We look forward to talking with you!
