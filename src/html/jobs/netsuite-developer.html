Netsuite Developer

CoLab is seeking an experienced Netsuite developer and integrator, who works well in a collaborative environment. We are a worker-owned cooperative specializing in building do-good technologies. This position offers an opportunity to work within a team and build technologies which help make the world a better place.

Experience and Requirements
  • Experience in NetSuite development, including SuiteScript (Javascript), SuiteFlow, and SuiteTalk.
  • Working knowledge of SuiteCloud Development Framework, REST Web Services, HTML and CSS, JSON.
  • Familiarity with NetSuite SuiteCloud development tools.
  • Familiarity with unit testing.
  • Prefer some experience in developing SuiteCommerce Advanced themes and extensions or (Backbone.js).
  • Experience in developing custom modules.
  • Fully comfortable using git.
  • Experience using support ticket management systems such as Zendesk or JIRA Service Desk.
  • Strong understanding of business processes and ability to translate requirements into technical solutions.
  • Ability to write maintainable, well-commented code.
  • Experience working with project management tools such as Redmine or Atlassian’s JIRA.
  • Strong organizational skills.
  • Team player (you’ll be part of the Delivery Team and work closely with PMs, designers, and other developers).
  • Strong professional communication skills (verbal and written).
  • A self starter with ability to manage and communicate timelines, scope, and budget.
  • Experience with time tracking and invoicing systems.
Other things that are helpful
  • Interest in a highly collaborative environment.
  • Driven to make the world better for everyone.
  • Experience working with remote, distributed teams.
  • Experience participating in team development processes and scrums
  • Familiarity with Jira, Trello, Google Apps, HipChat, and/or Freshbooks software is a plus.
  • Appreciation of simple beauty and clean code is a big plus.
  • A desire to work where you can bring your full self to your work.
  • A desire to grow and learn from and with others.
  • Empathy.
Time Commitment

This position is project based. It requires about 10 hours/week and can scale up to more hours and more projects for the right candidate. Our working hours are +/- 3 hours Eastern time zone. This initial engagement begins in December 2023 and the chosen candidate will be offered a contractual agreement on a quarterly basis with strong potential for long term work across a variety of clients. The ideal candidate is interested in pursuing membership with CoLab’s cooperative structure, but this is not a requirement.

Location Requirement

Preference may be given to candidates living in Alabama, California, New York, Ohio, Texas, Vermont, Wisconsin or outside of the U.S. Some travel for onsite client work may be required.

Our working hours are mainly +/- 3 hours Eastern time zone.

Compensation

Hourly, fairly based on experience and willingness to make the world a more just and peaceful place.

Benefits
  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly peers
  • Participate in mission-driven projects
  • Lots of room to grow
  • Educational resources
  • Path to ownership
  • Value-aligned community
  • Respect for work/life balance
About CoLab

CoLab was founded in 2010 as a worker-owned cooperative in order to co-create simple, purpose driven solutions to help solve global problems through collaboration and innovation. Our clients benefit from working with dedicated and talented technology team members who have been growing together for many years and who continue to form long lasting relationships with fellow workers, partners, and clients.

Ownership Candidacy

A path to ownership in the cooperative is open for the candidate. Details are based upon current membership criteria and subject to approval from the Board of Directors.

Apply

Please email jobs@colab.coop with the subject line “Netsuite Developer.” Please be prepared to share the following: a brief cover letter about your background and why you are looking for this kind of work, resume/CV, portfolio or professional links, writing samples, availability to start, and any questions you might have for us. We look forward to talking with you!
