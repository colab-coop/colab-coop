Bookkeeper

You are looking for a supportive, empowering environment. You are inspired to participate in a next economy business. You love flexibility and balancing work with life. You don’t ask for work, you find it. You’d rather solve a problem than worry about it. You are a black belt, in the precise art of bookkeeping. Oh, what’s that? That’s sounds like you? Sweet, let’s talk about applying those skills at the CoLab Cooperative...

The role of bookkeeper requires consistency and dependability. We are looking for someone who is focused on accurate and up to date numbers but also has interest in the bigger picture of optimizing our operations.

Required Skills
  • Must have an understanding of bookkeeping/accounting terms and best practices
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Good planning and organizational skills
  • Strong communication skills
  • Administrative skills to monitor financial data and interpret financial policies and procedures
  • Experience with FreshBooks and Quickbooks
Responsibilities
  • Payroll via Gusto, PayPal, ACH and with checks
  • Deposits
  • Payables
  • Cash flow monitoring using FloatApp
  • Weekly review of accounts aging
  • Expense tracking
  • Weekly reporting on cash position (screenshots from Float App and of expenses)
  • Billable hours + Profit Margin report (using premade Google Sheet templates)
  • Monthly financial report for the board of directors
  • Monthly reconciliation including the balance sheet, the profit/loss statement and expense report
Time Commitment

10-15 hours a week

Location

Flexible

Compensation

Commensurate with experience

Benefits
  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly peers
  • Participate in Do Good projects
  • Lots of room to grow
  • Educational resources
  • Stretch responsibilities that challenge you to level up!
  • Path to ownership
  • Value-aligned community
  • Respect for work / life balance
  • Support for mindfulness practices and spiritual development
Ownership Candidacy

A path to ownership is open for the candidate. Details are based upon current membership criteria and subject to approval from the Board of Directors.
