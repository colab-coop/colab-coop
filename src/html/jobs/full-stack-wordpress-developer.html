Full-Stack WordPress Developer

CoLab Cooperative is seeking a Full-Stack WordPress Developer to join our team. As a Full-Stack WordPress Developer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining WordPress websites and working closely with cross-functional teams to ensure that websites meet user needs and align with business objectives. The ideal candidate must have a solid understanding of web design principles, be proficient in PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have experience working with WordPress and related technologies.

Key Responsibilities

To succeed in this role, you will be required to:

  • Implement website features
  • Work with designers to make decisions and implement styles
  • Create custom themes
  • Create custom plugins
  • Optimize website performance
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Manage website hosting and server configurations
  • Integrate third-party services and APIs
  • Provide technical support to clients or team members
Requirements

To be considered for this role, the ideal candidate must have:

  • At least 5 years of recent experience working on WordPress development projects as the technical lead or sole developer
  • Experience creating WordPress themes from scratch to match professional design mockups created with Figma, Adobe XD, or similar
  • Expertise in front-end technologies, including JavaScript, CSS3, and HTML5, and third-party libraries such as React Js, Angular, and jQuery
  • Knowledge of CSS tools such as PostCSS or SASS
  • Knowledge of CSS frameworks such as Tailwind, Bootstrap, or Foundation
  • Experience creating CSS layouts using Flexbox or CSS Grid
  • Experience creating custom Gutenberg blocks
  • Recent experience using Elementor Pro (version 3.0 and up) as a full-site editor
  • Experience developing custom plugins
  • Understanding of using WordPress hooks (filters and actions)
  • Enqueuing of stylesheets and scripts
  • Familiarity with the Roots Bedrock stack for using Composer with WordPress
  • Experience with MySQL (bonus points if you’ve worked with MariaDB too)
  • Experience with WordPress multisite
The following additional skills will also be highly valued:
  • Knowledge of WP-CLI
  • Experience with NPM, Github, Bitbucket, and Docker
  • Familiarity with React, Django, or Ember.js
  • Experience with ticket management systems such as JIRA, Basecamp, or Asana
  • Knowledge of Google Workspace or Office 365
  • Understanding of Scalable Vector Graphics
Moreover, the ideal candidate will:
  • Know when to use a plugin and when not to
  • Know the right plugin to use, how to research options, and make a good recommendation
  • Be experienced with different types of integrations including: LMS (LearnDash), eCommerce (WooCommerce), Fundraising platforms (GiveWP), Advanced Search (SearchWP, FacetWP), CRM (CiviCRM, EveryAction, Hubspot, etc.), Mailchimp, Constant Contact
  • Have agency experience
  • Have experience working as part of an Agile team
  • Have experience running a web development business
  • Have experience working with nonprofit organizations
  • Be able to do complex technical estimations and write proposals
  • Have strong client communication skills, including the ability to build rapport, listen carefully, analyze requirements, and make sound recommendations with confidence
  • Have strong troubleshooting skills and be able to solve complex problems
  • Know how to keep a WordPress site secure, best practices around backups, etc.
  • Have experience working with advanced content architecture using custom post types, taxonomies, and metaboxes.
  • Know the WordPress template hierarchy
  • Have a strong understanding about SEO
  • Know about Accessibility
  • Is able to keep up with changes in the industry
Time Commitment

Part time and project based. Estimated to be 15 hours a week to start and increasing (if desired) over time.

Location Requirement

Flexible. This is a remote position.

Compensation

Commensurate with experience, based on CoLab's standard rate scale.

Benefits
  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly peers
  • Participate in meaningful and mission-oriented projects
  • Lots of room to grow
  • Stretch responsibilities that challenge you to level up!
  • Path to ownership with additional benefits coming with membership (PTO, Parental leave, bereavement leave, patronage, participation in governance, and more)
  • Value-aligned community
  • Respect for work / life balance
