Project Manager

You don’t ask for work, you find it. You’re not screaming for attention, you’re looking out for others on the team. You’d rather solve a problem than worry about it. You are a black belt, in the ancient skill of Project Management. Oh, what’s that? That’s sounds like you? Sweet, let’s talk about applying those skills at the CoLab Cooperative...

The role of Project Manager is multifaceted - much like spinning a bunch of plates (that are on fire) simultaneously. The position requires the ability to interact professionally in writing or on the phone with our clients. This position requires someone who is very organized and detail oriented. This position also requires someone who can recognize possible roadblocks or issues and work to resolve them before they get out of hand.

Experience and requirements
  • 2-5 years experience with project management for web design and development or similar
  • Available and 'on call' when needed per contractor agreement
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Self starter
  • Team player (you’ll be part of the Delivery Team and work closely with fellow PMs)
  • Strong professional communication skills (verbal and written)
  • Interest in highly collaborative environment
  • Driven to make the world better for everyone
  • Experience with project and task management systems
  • Experience with time tracking and invoicing systems
  • Ability to manage client expectations and communicate about timelines, scope and budget
Other things that are helpful
  • Experience facilitating agile development processes and scrums
  • Technical knowledge related to web and mobile application development
  • Familiarity with Jira, Trello, Google Apps, HipChat, and Freshbooks software a plus
  • Keen design eye and appreciation of simple beauty a big plus
Role and Responsibilities
  • Facilitation of team check-ins / scrums and communications
  • Skillful communication with clients
  • Budget tracking
  • Milestone tracking
  • Unblock critical work through proactive communication and project vision
  • Tracking your team’s capacity
  • Help with documenting project requirements
  • Ability to support and help with QA as needed
  • Attend weekly Delivery Team meetings to update process of project(s)
Time Commitment

10-20 hours a week to start

Location Requirement

Flexible

Compensation

Commensurate with experience

Benefits
  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly peers
  • Participate in Do Good projects
  • Lots of room to grow
  • Educational resources
  • Stretch responsibilities that challenge you to level up!
  • Path to ownership
  • Value-aligned community
  • Respect for work / life balance
  • Support for spiritual growth
Interested?

Please be share a resume, CV and / or some writing about your background and why you are looking for this kind of work to core@colab.coop.
