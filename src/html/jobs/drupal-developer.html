Drupal Developer

CoLab is seeking an experienced Drupal developer who works well in a collaborative environment. We are a worker-owned cooperative specializing in building do good technologies. This position offers an opportunity to work within a team and build technologies which help make the world a better place.

Role and Responsibilities
  • Implement site specifications such as wireframes, IA documentation and user stories using the Drupal CMS.
  • Work with clients and project managers to identify the best modules, tools and architecture to meet client needs.
  • Create and modify custom themes as per design comps and guidelines.
  • Respond to all team and client communications in a timely manner.
  • Be on time and present for all scheduled team and client meetings.
  • Provide recommendations for how CoLab teams’ development processes can be iterated on and improved.
Experience and requirements
  • Minimum of 3-4 years experience developing Drupal sites
  • Thorough knowledge of Drupal 7 site building best practices, common modules and theming standards.
  • Familiarity with feature-based development for content type configuration, panels setup, etc.
  • Some experience developing custom modules.
  • Knowledge of modern HTML & CSS standards, including responsive web development and semantic markup.
  • Comfort working with basic JavaScript and jQuery, including custom theme JavaScript and Drupal JS settings arrays.
  • Ability to write maintainable, well-commented code.
  • Experience working with project management tools such as Redmine or Atlassian’s JIRA.
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Self starter
  • Team player (you’ll be part of the Delivery Team and work closely with PMs, designers and other developers)
  • Strong professional communication skills (verbal and written)
  • Ability to manage and communicate timelines, scope and budget
  • Experience with time tracking and invoicing systems
  • Fully comfortable using git
Other things that are helpful
  • Interest in highly collaborative environment
  • Driven to make the world better for everyone
  • Experience working in a remote, distributed team a bonus
  • Experience participated in team development processes and scrums
  • Technical knowledge related to web and mobile application development.
  • Experience with Drupal 8.
  • Involvement in one or more open source projects, especially Drupal-related.
  • Familiarity with Jira, Trello, Google Apps, HipChat, and/or Freshbooks software a plus
  • Appreciation of simple beauty and clean code a big plus
  • A desire to work where you can bring your full self to your work
  • Empathy
  • A desire to grow and learn from and with others
Time Commitment

Project based 15-20 hours a week to start

Location Requirement

Flexible

Compensation

Commensurate with experience

Benefits
  • Flexible schedule
  • Friendly peers
  • Participate in Do Good projects
  • Lots of room to grow
  • Educational resources
  • Stretch responsibilities that challenge you to level up!
  • Path to ownership
  • Value-aligned community
  • Respect for work / life balance
  • Support for mindfulness practices and spiritual development
Ownership Candidacy

A path to ownership is open for the candidate. Details are based upon current membership criteria and subject to approval from the Board of Directors.
