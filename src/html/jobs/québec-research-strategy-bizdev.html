Québec Research, Strategy, & Business Development Role

la version Française, cliquez ici

CoLab is working to develop a stronger role in contributing to the co-op development and knowledge building movement in Québec. We’re passionate to support the next generation of cooperators starting and growing social impact businesses that benefit people and planet. CoLab is seeking a motivated, self-starter, of entrepreneurial spirit who would like to work with us to grow our Québec office into a more formal contributor to efforts underway with partners and allies on the ground such in Montreal.

Does that sound like you? If so, let’s talk about bringing those skills to CoLab Cooperative!

About CoLab

CoLab is an international, worker-owned tech collective that builds interactive technologies for a more just and sustainable planet. Our co-op was founded in 2010 to co-create simple, purpose-driven solutions to global problems. We are committed to our clients for the long haul, and we bring our entire team to every engagement. We run a very open, flat, and collaborative organization, and invest in one another as friends and coworkers. Our co-op has a path to becoming a member-ownership, which we hope you’ll consider.

Who We’re Looking For

CoLab coworkers bring a wide range of talent and perspectives to our collaboration. We’re looking for people who try to meet complexity with simplicity and can communicate about it with their peers. As a co-op, we embrace both autonomy and mutual aid. You’ll have clear responsibilities in your role, as well as opportunities to contribute in other areas. We have no criteria around particular background or credentials; we care about your proposals, what you produce, and your growth and happiness with our team. If CoLab sounds like a place for you, read on!

Your Responsibilities
  • Work as part of a team to generate qualified leads through business networking with organizations and partners
  • Help us tailor our business service offering and overall business development strategy to the needs of co-operative and social impact partners in Québec.
  • Be our liaison of introduction to aligned partners, co-workers, allies, and partners who share our mission and principles to build a transnational solidarity economy, together.
  • Work with the Business Development team to co-author proposals to aligned regional partners
  • Generate and close on new business opportunities for CoLab in Québec
  • Conduct research and analysis on new work opportunities as part of Discovery scopes of work intended to lead to larger bodies of work.
  • Help co-design technology solutions that meet the needs of our customers and allies’ needs.
  • Contribute strategic thinking around how we can better serve our clients and community partners.
What you’ll need
  • 3+ years working in the strategic planning and design of software solutions ideally using open source technologies.
  • Strong domain knowledge and good relationships with contributors.
  • Experience in digital and interactive product development
  • Ability to conduct needs assessments, stakeholder analysis, market research
  • Strategic thinking for proposing solutions that meet needs
  • Familiarity with the co-op movement, principles, and organizations in Québec, Canada and with the approaches needed to organize across North America
  • Experience with organizing and leading grassroots movements including tech-focused movements
  • Familiarity with social impact models and organizations in Québec
  • Commitment to social impact and economic justice work vis a vis SDG goals
  • Desire to build solidarity, align needs and work across nations and regions
    • CA <> US
  • Ability and willingness to travel as necessary
  • Empathic communication skills
  • French and English language fluency is required
Other things that are helpful
  • Experience as a member of a cooperative
  • Prior experience with pitches/fundraising and presentations
  • Experience recruiting and supporting local meetup organizers.
  • Comfort working with a remote or distributed team
  • Research skills
  • Need assessment, stakeholder analysis skills
  • Media relations, networking in CA, US, startup community
  • Entrepreneurial thinking
  • Desire to support youth education and mentorship initiatives
Location Requirement

This position is open to applicants living in Québec with a preference to hire an applicant from Montreal given our community there.

Compensation

Retainer +hourly. On retainer basis for business development and strategy efforts with performance measured against to-be-shared key performance indicators.

Research for client billable subcontract work paid on an hourly basis.

Time Commitment

10 hours per week to start, focusing on closing a new work contract with ICA.coop and its partners. Opportunity to grow from this project as a research contributor and strategist to a multi-year engagement supporting Knowledge Building and Youth Mentorship in Québec.

Key Dates

Anticipated start date July 1, 2019.

Apply

Please email jobs@colab.coop with the subject line “Québec Research and Strategy Candidate.”

We center underrepresented people and communities in our work. Women, gender non-conforming people, and people of color strongly encouraged to apply.

Please be prepared to share the following:

  • A brief, 2-3 paragraph cover letter about your background and what you hope to bring to this work
  • A resume or CV and portfolio or professional links
  • Availability to start and other considerations
  • Two questions you have for us

We look forward to talking with you!
