Data Analyst / CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) Specialist

CoLab is looking to collaborate with someone experienced in data analysis to work together on large scale social impact projects in service to clients and causes we hold dear. We are looking for someone who thinks deeply about online engagement and the analysis of patterns of action.

Roles and Responsibilities:
Data Analyst
  • Review inbound traffic/GA reports and highlight important trends and/or data points
  • Configure basic goals
  • Understand Google Tag Manager and it’s role in analytics
  • Use segmentation and date selector to provide insightful analysis into client traffic over time and/or between channels
Conversion rate optimization
  • Set up and run A/B tests with some modest complexity using tools like Visual Website Optimizer
  • User analytics and competitor research to highlight testable hypotheses
  • Recommend approaches to improving conversion on key on-site engagement
  • Articulate tests clearly to client and front end designer
  • Understand basics of statistical significance for tests and be able to communicate them to client
  • Help us tell the story of a user visiting a site or application
Strategist
  • You understand our goals and want to help us achieve them
Business Strategist
  • You consider bottom line sustainability in your recommendations as well as support for other KPIs
Possible Other Roles:

PPC:
  • Familiarity in setting up and running effective Adwords campaigns
Leader:
  • You are ready to chart a collaborative path forward in your areas of expertise

About CoLab Cooperative

Our Vision: Through prototyping democracy together in the workplace, we can improve democratic processes and networks of governance in the world more broadly.

Our Values:

  • Collaboration (duh)
  • Passion
  • Wisdom
  • Innovation
  • Sustainability

CoLab was founded in 2010 as a worker-owned cooperative in order to use technology to shape a brighter future.

CoLab designs and develops technology for mission driven organizations and entrepreneurs. We have team members around the world. The diversity of our members’ backgrounds and geographies makes CoLab unique in our collaboration approach and value delivered.

CoLab’s mission is to contribute to a equitable and sustainable economy. When fully aligned, our work amplifies the impact and accelerates the growth of our partners.

Together we co-create mission-driven technologies to solve problems, amplify good deeds, and shape a brighter future.

Cooperatives and Membership

CoLab was founded with a clear path to membership for all it’s workers. As part of working together, we will introduce you to how our co-op operates. We will collaborate together in a distributed manner with different members and community around the world. Hopefully you will come to value that democracy prototyping that is our work as our intention for those we work to with be interested in participating in co-operative models of governance.
