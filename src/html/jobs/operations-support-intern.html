Operations Support Intern

CoLab is seeking an organized, enthusiastic and quick-to-learn individual to support our day-to-day operations and help us continually evolve our do-good tech cooperative so we can help more people!

The Ops Support collaborator is an entry-level position focused on maintaining and improving our core business activities: facilitating our operations, accounting and other teams with process, secretarial and coordination support, and generally being a helping hand for all sorts of internal projects and duties.

Required Skills
  • You’ll need to be comfortable with basic workplace softwares such as word processing, spreadsheets and presentation software. We use Google Docs.
  • You’ll need to be able to handle a moderate volume of email and requests with prompt responses.
  • You’ll need to be comfortable managing your outstanding tasks and priorities using an online tool. We use JIRA and a wiki, but experience with some computer task list (even a text file) is important.
  • You’ll need to have organizational skills and an enthusiasm for systems. A lot of the work you’ll be helping with is helping others keep organizational systems working and helping to improve those systems over time. Some experience with this sort of work and a disposition toward keeping things in order will be critical to your success.
  • It would help for you to have some experience with online collaboration tools, such as wikis, forums, or groupware (like Basecamp). These systems are core to our work, and you can learn the specifics on the job, but some background will set you up well.
  • Familiarity with web communication platforms — such as Twitter, Facebook and Youtube — is also a plus. The world we work in is built upon major web platforms, and understanding how to use them yourself and knowing some of the strategy behind how groups can best make use of them will position you well to interface with clients and project teams.
  • Knowing some web technologies, such as basic HTML or CSS, is not required, but it would sure be cool.
  • Knowledge of Quickbooks would be great.
Responsibilities
  • Support the accounting team in generation of invoices and tracking of project budgets.
  • Help manage incoming payments and logging of expenses: cashing checks, logging in QuickBooks, and tracking in our key financial spreadsheets.
  • Support the operations team in key projects: helping with scheduling, coordination and execution on systems improvement, creation and maintenance tasks.
  • Keeping the capacity planning worksheet up to date: getting all collaborators to fill out their capacity and making sure projects are allocated week by week.
  • Secretarial support: organizing key online documents, documenting operating procedures, keeping documentation up to date, etc.
  • Facilities support: help with straightening, prettying and coordinating the Ithaca office and Hive co-working space.
Example Task List

An example of tasks you might be asked to work on:

  • Weekly Operation Projection Cash Flow update
  • Draft invoice creation for bi-monthly pay periods
  • Assistance in “Accounting Bible” creation, updating, refining
  • B-Corp application
  • Help bringing clients over to Google Apps
  • Help with MT migration
  • Hive Office upkeep assistance: water jug, trash, recycling, plants.
  • Weekly Digest creation
  • CoLab social media assistance, if interested
  • Maintaining member-owner benefits tracking
  • Wiki Gardening for CoLab confluence space
Time Commitment

10-20 hours a week to start.

Location

Ithaca, NY

Compensation

Commensurate with experience.

Interested?

Send your cover letter and LinkedIn or resume to core@colab.coop.
