Corey Keating

Developer
-->

Corey's passion for music and technology stems back to his early experiments sequencing live performances on a Commodore Amiga and mangling audio samples in an old, unreliable RT 6150 computer. An ardent lover of the outdoors and an avid mycophile, Corey finds constant sources of creative inspiration in the natural world.

He is currently doing doctoral work at Cornell University with an emphasis on experimental and electronic music, and has previously held positions with the Melos Music Collective and the Ira F. Brilliant Center for Beethoven Studies, where he was assistant editor. His interest in productive methodologies and work-flow enhancement has led to an ongoing examination of the psychological principles behind motivation and creativity, and has brought him a renewed sense of appreciation for the nature of collaborative partnerships.
