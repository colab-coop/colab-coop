Rylan has been working in the technology industry since the late 90s when he graduated from Stanford University with a degree focus on ecological sustainability and with a personal mission to develop solutions that address the need for regeneration of the earth, its communities, and its ecosystems.

He began this journey by understanding the capital needs of social impact technology startup ventures while working at a Silicon Valley venture capital firm in order to better understand the incubation and acceleration of technology. From there, Rylan embarked on a long journey as an entrepreneur working through the various aspects of social enterprise development from co-founding a startup, GiveBack, to consulting on business strategy, to rolling up his sleeves as a front-end web engineer. With help from peers, Rylan converted one of these early technology experiments he co-founded into an early form of the co-operative that CoLab is today.

Today, Rylan collaborates with mission-driven entrepreneurs and organizations to co-create a brighter future one human interaction at a time. Rylan serves as CoLab's senior business strategist and an active product design contributor.
