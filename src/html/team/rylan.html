Rylan began working in the technology industry during the late 90s when he graduated from Stanford University and began a stint in tech venture capital. Compelled to get his hands deeper in development, Rylan subsequently worked as a front-end engineer, designing and coding user interfaces for enterprise clients. On the basis of the experience, Rylan co-founded CoLab Cooperative, a web development agency that collaborates with its clients to co-create a brighter future. Today Rylan serves as CoLab's senior business strategist.
