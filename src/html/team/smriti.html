Smriti Gupta

Project Manager

CONNECT

Smriti has over 12 years of experience leading enterprise applications teams and projects. She graduated from Delhi University with a bachelor of science in mathematics and post graduate in computer applications. Smriti is highly process-oriented, having spent years as a project manager in structured organizations before joining CoLab.

As a project manager, Smriti was responsible for many milestones for Nokia Siemens Networks, including a rebranding initiative and GSM customer documentation. Her attention to detail and love of process, combined with an effective communication style, make her an indispensable asset to the CoLab project management team.

Smriti was drawn to the work flexibility afforded by CoLab's collaborative model, a concept she is glad she explored. In her spare time, Smriti enjoys expanding her project management skills and playing guitar.
