Jenn Schlick

Jenn graduated with honors from the University of Maine with a bachelor of arts in journalism and English. She joined Carbonite during its first years, designing websites and marketing campaigns. At the digital agency Echo & Co., she was fortunate to have a hand in creating technology solutions for social good. These values have been an important part of her work ever since.

At CoLab, Jenn enjoys being challenged and challenging others to do their best work. She finds immense value in being part of a close-knit team that pushes each other forward.
