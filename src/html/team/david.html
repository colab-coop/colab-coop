David is a technology enthusiast, futurist, and programmer. He is passionate about simplicity, collaboration, egalitarianism, and a more aware, compassionate world. He believes we are now at a crucial stage in human societal evolution where positive, radical change for good can be amplified and extended in ways never before possible.

David is a founding member of the Maker’s Local 256 hackerspace in Huntsville, Alabama. He enjoys hiking, sailing, meditation, and vegetarian cooking. He hopes to help encourage a more DIY, participatory, and community-centered world.
