CONNECT

Colleen serves multiple roles at CoLab. She is Team Org Area Co-Lead, a role to which she brings social & emotional skill as well as an ever-deepening knowledge in collaboration dynamics. In this capacity, she acts as facilitator, mediator, and coach. She also collaborates in policy creation. Her goal is to foster psychological safety within the CoLab team, and help to create more joy, creativity and innovation in the workplace.

As a project manager, Colleen brings attentiveness, passion, and care to her projects and teams. She works to foster a sense of shared purpose within project teams and to create a space where everyone can bring their skills and gifts to the fullest expression possible. More than organizing timelines and budgets, Colleen brings a high level of curiosity and strategic thinking to her work as a project manager.

Colleen believes in a regenerative economy, where business can be part of social, economic, and climate healing. She is also passionate about democratic workplaces and is an advocate for the global cooperative movement.
