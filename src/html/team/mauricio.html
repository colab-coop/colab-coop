Mauricio is a lover of languages, both computer and human. In addition to his native Spanish, he is fluent in English and PHP, and currently studies French and Javascript. His love for computers began in high school. Since then he has received a bachelors degree in computer science, graduating with the highest GPA in his class. Later he went on to earn a MBA, exemplifying his ability to aid clients in overall business strategy.

Mauricio is the Nicaraguan Drupal community lead. He promotes the adoption of drupal in his country by organizing free trainings and presenting in tech events. Mauricio contributes significantly to his local community, but he also has a strong presence in the international tech community, having worked for companies all over the world including USA, Germany, Mexico, Peru, and India. He has been involved in large projects for clients such as the Inter-American Development Bank, National Retail Federation, Seventh Generation, and Bunn-o-Matic Corporation.

Mauricio hopes to leverage his passions and talents to do good in the world.
