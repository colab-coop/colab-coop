Michał Kuklis

Developer

CONNECT

Michał has been working as a software developer since 2004. He has experience with various web development tools and frameworks. He enjoys experimenting with new technologies.

Michał received a B.S. from Poznan University of Technology in Computer Science and a M.Eng. from Cornell University in Computer Science. Since 2009 he has been working on various projects for small businesses, startups and bigger corporations. His primary focus is on web and mobile work.

He was drawn to Colab by their cooperative structure and their values.

In his free time Michał enjoys playing soccer, traveling and exploring new countries and cultures, and learning about new approaches in software development.
