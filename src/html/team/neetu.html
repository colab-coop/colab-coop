Neetu Morwani

Developer

CONNECT

Neetu Morwani is a talented Drupal developer with a keen eye for quality code and client communication. With a background as an engineer and extensive working experience with Drupal 7 and 8, Neetu has earned a “Grand Master” Drupal certification and uses this knowledge to mentor and contribute to developer communities. She’s spoken at DrupalCon Asia.

Neetu grew up in Jaipur, the capital and largest city of Rajasthan in Northern India. Such an upbringing, she says, included the best of both tradition and modernity, providing a mix of technology and nature, calmness and energy. To this end, she often gardens and reads, while also travelling regularly.

With an appreciation of CoLab’s support for progressive organizations, Neetu stands behind organizations such as Women Empowerment in India.
