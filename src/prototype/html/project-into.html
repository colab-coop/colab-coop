Online Storytelling Platform for the Green Economy

Cross-platform   Business

  • Native iOS mobile application and web platform with extensive UX / Design
  • Custom code scanning technology to replace QR codes
  • Business development support with a sales MVP
  • Content focused design process
  • Mobile and web UX

INTO came to CoLab with a bold challenge: To be the preeminent mobile storytelling platform for the green economy. With plans to target customers like Whole Foods, Fair Trade USA, and Sambazon, we were excited to help the company get traction from their MVP product while building an enterprise grade application, CMS and API that could meet the needs of their enterprise clients for the long-term.

In order to provide the best possible opportunity for success CoLab brought together a cross-disciplinary team that included:

  • UX / UI design talent for mobile / web
  • Native iOS application development
  • Web API development experience
  • Front-end coding engineering
  • Strategic business consulting expertise calling on our management team's experience in the venture capital.

Success came quickly for INTO once CoLab began working with the client as the INTO team began sharing the initial sales minimum viable product at trade-shows while setting up one on one meetings with the most successful companies of the green economy even securing pre-sales commitments to the product offering while still under development. By helping the client to establish market and product fit quickly and definitively, CoLab helped the client to successfully raise additional capital and further invest in its product development and market roll-out strategy.

Part of the beauty of this project was how CoLab was able to take an idea with great technical and business complexity and distill it down to an elegant technical solution that was easy to use and emotionally compelling. Companies quickly understood the power of the INTO product in helping them to share their stories with their customers, and the company was quickly tracked for success from there.

How did we architect a solution for them?

  • Authentication using bearer tokens
  • JSON over REST APIs serving both iOS app and web CMS
  • Modular server over Node.js
  • S3 integration to store all the media files
  • CMS built using AngularJS
